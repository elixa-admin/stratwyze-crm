# Sales Lifecycle Phases 14-16: Guided Journey Design

> **Waves 14-16 Roadmap**: Transform discovery, qualification, and proposal generation from manual work to AI-guided workflows.

---

## 🎯 OVERVIEW

The sales rep's journey from lead to proposal should require **minimal manual input** while maximizing intel at each stage:

```
PHASE 14: Lead Intake → Discovery       (Prepare sales rep for conversation)
PHASE 15: Discovery → Qualification    (Validate fit, prepare to advance)
PHASE 16: Qualified → Proposal         (Build sales pitch, close the deal)
```

Each phase has:
- **Guided workflow** (rep is stepped through key questions)
- **AI-powered intelligence** (research, battle-cards, scoring auto-loaded)
- **Actionable recommendations** (go/no-go, positioning, next steps)
- **Handoff to next stage** (all context preserved for next phase)

---

## PHASE 14: LEAD INTAKE → GUIDED DISCOVERY

**Duration**: Pre-meeting setup → Discovery call → Post-call logging (3-5 days)

**Goal**: Sales rep walks into discovery call with:
- ✅ Pre-meeting brief (company intel, pain points)
- ✅ Incumbent platform identified (if possible)
- ✅ 5-7 tailored discovery questions
- ✅ Stakeholder map
- ✅ Battle-card ready (if incumbent is guessed)
