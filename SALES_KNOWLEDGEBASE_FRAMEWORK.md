## 4. DISCOVERY FRAMEWORKS

### ITSM Fit Discovery (Pre-Meeting)

**Company Profile Questions:**
- Organization size (employees, locations)
- Industry and vertical
- Current ITSM maturity (ad-hoc, managed, optimized)
- Number of IT staff, team structure
- Geographic distribution

**Incumbent Platform Questions:**
- What platform are they running today?
- How long have they been on it?
- Who's the implementer/maintainer?
- Are they satisfied? If not, what are the pain points?
- Are they in a refresh/RFP cycle?

**Pain Point Map:**
- **Operational**: Ticket backlog, SLA miss rate, MTTR, user adoption
- **Financial**: License costs, implementation costs, hidden costs (add-ons, training)
- **Organizational**: Team productivity, change request delays, knowledge gaps
- **Strategic**: Scalability for growth, roadmap alignment, innovation

**Decision Criteria (MEDDIC variant for ITSM):**
- **Metrics**: What success looks like (faster tickets, lower costs, better adoption)
- **Economic Buyer**: CFO, VP IT, CIO (budget holder)
- **Decision Process**: RFP, sole-source, steering committee, board approval
- **Decision Criteria**: Platform features, cost, implementation speed, vendor stability
- **Identify Pain**: What's broken today that needs fixing
- **Champion**: Internal advocate for change (usually VP IT or IT Manager)
