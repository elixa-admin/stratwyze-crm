What We Are

AISP is an AI-native consulting agency. We replace traditional hierarchies with autonomous AI agents guided by human strategic judgment.

We are building a 14-person autonomous organization:

  • 1 Human CEO (you) — Strategic decisions, Wave planning, company vision
  • 4 AI Directors — CTO, CPO, CSO, CFO — Team leadership, KPI ownership, boundary decisions
  • 8 AI Agents — Specialized execution across engineering, product, sales, finance
  • 1 AI Coordinator — Cross-team orchestration

Unlike traditional consulting, we don't hire more people as you scale. We replicate the agent architecture across projects. Same 14-person structure, infinite projects.

What We're Building

Three-wave roadmap to proven, scalable autonomous model:

Wave 1: Foundation (Now → Feb 2027)

Build one autonomous org: AISP Portal with 14-person structure.

What gets deployed: Org consolidation, automated agent creation, agent loops, 3 managed agents, smart pattern library.

Target: 95% autonomy, 30% token savings, proven internal operation.

Wave 2: Multi-Project (Feb → Oct 2027)

Replicate structure: 3-5 projects under AISP umbrella, same org model.

What gets built: Cross-project pattern sharing, expanded agent teams.

Target: 98% autonomy, 40% token savings, scalable model proven.

Wave 3+: Scale (Oct 2027+)

Proven repeatable pattern: Unlimited projects with same structure.

Business options: Continue as internal agency, license the model, sell the platform.

Target: 99%+ autonomy, 50%+ token savings, model ready for market.
